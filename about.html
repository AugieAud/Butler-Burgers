About us

Our Story

Butler Burgers was founded in 2015 by Chef James Butler, who had a simple mission: to create the perfect burger. After years of working in fine dining establishments, James wanted to bring gourmet techniques and quality ingredients to the humble burger.

What started as a small food truck has now grown into a beloved restaurant with a loyal following. We still maintain the same commitment to quality and taste that made our burgers famous from day one.

Our Philosophy

At Butler Burgers, we believe that a great burger starts with great ingredients. We source our beef from local farms where cattle are raised humanely and without antibiotics or hormones. Our produce is locally grown and delivered fresh daily.

We make our sauces and condiments in-house, and our buns are baked fresh each morning. It's this attention to detail and commitment to quality that sets our burgers apart.

Our Team

Our team is made up of passionate food lovers who take pride in creating the perfect burger experience for our customers. From our chefs to our servers, everyone at Butler Burgers is committed to providing exceptional food and service.

We're like a family here, and we extend that warm, welcoming atmosphere to everyone who walks through our doors. We hope you'll come join us for a meal soon!
