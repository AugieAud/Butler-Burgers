About us

Our Story

We have been in the food service business for 85 years and counting, spanning three generations and doing everything from fast food to full service.

Along the way, we have been incredibly blessed to build a loyal following and lasting friendships. Our customers continue to trust us year after year to provide family friendly service and good quality food.

Maggie's Legacy

The inspiration behind our dog menu comes from our girl Maggie, who gave us unconditional love for 16 beautiful years ❤️ and absolutely loved her treats.

She would help me in the kitchen at home making these dog bone cookies and was always the first to approve them, warm out of the oven! She made our family complete and our lives better.

Thank You

My sincere gratitude goes to our devoted staff, who for so many years have believed in our philosophy of building friendships, hospitality, and lasting memories. Many of them have been with us for 10, 15, even 25 years!

A special thank you goes to Jimmy, whose endless commitment and positive drive brought this venture to life. Without him, none of this may have been possible. He is also the creative force behind all the delicious recipes on our Butler Burger menu!

Most importantly, a big thank you to our family, friends, and loyal customers.

Thank you for supporting our local family business.
